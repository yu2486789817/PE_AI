# Project Overview Statement (POS)

## English Version

| Field | Content |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Project Name** | Intelligent Classroom Platform for University Physical Education (PE AI Manager) |
| **Project No.** | PE-AI-2026-01 |
| **Project Manager** | [Your Name] |
| **Problem/Opportunity** | Traditional university physical education lacks personalized feedback outside of class time. Teachers struggle to manually grade large amounts of video homework for physical movements. There is a significant opportunity to leverage AI video analysis (e.g., YOLO) to automatically evaluate student postures, correct movements in real-time, and generate intelligent feedback. This will dramatically improve teaching efficiency and student learning outcomes. |
| **Goal** | To implement an intelligent physical education platform by the end of the current academic semester that integrates AI automated video analysis, course management, and homework tracking, enabling efficient teaching and self-guided physical training for university students. |
| **Objectives** | 1. Develop a comprehensive web platform with dedicated interfaces for both teachers and students.<br>2. Implement AI-driven video analysis to automatically evaluate specific student movements (e.g., basketball shooting joint angles, swimming postures) and provide instant, automated feedback.<br>3. Integrate a course and homework management system allowing teachers to smoothly assign and review video-based physical tasks.<br>4. Deploy an AI chat assistant to support students and teachers with course-related queries and technical usage. |
| **Success Criteria** | 1. The AI model correctly identifies and analyzes specific sports movements with an accuracy of at least 85%.<br>2. The system reduces the time teachers spend grading physical education homework by at least 50%.<br>3. The backend successfully processes concurrent video uploads and AI analysis requests for at least 300 students without performance failure.<br>4. At least 90% of the assigned video homework receives automated AI feedback within 60 seconds of upload. |
| **Assumptions, Risks, Obstacles** | 1. **Assumptions:** Students have access to smartphones with adequate cameras to record video homework; the campus network can handle large video uploads.<br>2. **Risks:** The accuracy of the AI analysis model may fluctuate due to poor video quality, environmental lighting, or severe camera shaking by students.<br>3. **Obstacles:** The integration between the deep learning models (YOLO/Python microservices) and the Spring Boot backend might face high latency or performance bottlenecks during peak submission hours. |
